About Me

My name is Emily Buickerood and I am a marketing manager/graphic designer, aspiring developer, music lover, coffee addict, avid traveler, horse owner, and self-proclaimed bagel snob.

I graduated from Philadelphia University in May 2012 with a Bachelors Degree in Graphic Design Communication and have since continued my education by pursing and completing web development courses throughout 2017.

By day, I am the marketing manager of a veterinary management company where I manage, design and aid in the development of all 36 of our partner's websites as well as their print marketing and email marketing materials. I handle our in-house design work and website management as well.

By night, I am a freelance designer who enjoys creating websites and print materials for companies and organizations around Philadelphia as well as smaller, more personal projects. I have worked with many members of the food truck industry, small businesses and non-profits, and have created invitations for many types of occasions including bridal showers and weddings.

Outside of design, you can find me traveling somewhere new, trying restaurants around Philadelphia, or relaxing on the beach. I am constantly in search of a new adventure and always looking for new opportunities!
